| value           | Description                                     |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| credit_card     | Payment was made by credit card                 |
| bank_transfer   | Payment was made by bank transfer               |
| gift_card       | Order was paid by redeeming a gift card         |
| coupon          | Order was obtained in exchange for a coupon     |
